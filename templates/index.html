ScribeSense:

S

Traditional grading methods are often time-consuming and lack the ability to provide nuanced feedback, especially for large datasets. Existing automated solutions often struggle with diverse writing styles and synonyms, leading to subjective assessments. This project presents ScribeSense, a novel approach to automated evaluation that utilizes a comprehensive database of vectorized answer representations and leverages Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques with a Large Language Model (LLM). ScribeSense analyzes student responses in .docx format, compares them to the relevant vectorized answer in the database, and calculates a score based on semantic similarity. This ensures objective and consistent grading. Additionally, the integrated LLM generates personalized feedback for students, highlighting areas for improvement and suggesting alternative phrasings, fostering deeper understanding and exceeding the limitations of static grading rubrics.

How to use:-

1. Teacher has to register on the web app and then upload a document (in .docx format) with correct answers.

2. Student has to register on the web app and then upload a document (in .docx format) with his solutions.
